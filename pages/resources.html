Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from festival programmers, distributors, and educators.

CineFrame is available in multiple formats including DCP for theatrical screenings, ProRes 422 HQ for broadcast, H.264 MP4 for digital platforms, and Blu-ray for physical distribution. We can provide custom formats upon request with 48-hour notice.

Festival screenings and educational use are generally unrestricted. Commercial distribution requires separate licensing agreements. Press usage of promotional materials is permitted with proper attribution. Contact us for specific licensing requirements.

Our educational package includes production breakdowns, animation technique explanations, curriculum integration guides, student worksheets, and discussion questions. Materials are available for high school and college level instruction.

Festival programmers can request screening copies through our contact form below. Please include festival details, screening dates, and preferred format. We typically respond within 24 hours and can provide materials within 48 hours.
