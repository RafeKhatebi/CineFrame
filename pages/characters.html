Animation Techniques

Discover the technical artistry and animation principles that bring our characters to life on screen.

Squash & Stretch

Fundamental principle used to give characters weight and flexibility, making movements feel organic and believable.

Anticipation

Strategic preparation movements that telegraph actions, helping audiences follow the story and character intentions.

Timing & Spacing

Precise control of movement speed and rhythm to convey personality, emotion, and physical properties of characters.

Secondary Action

Subtle supporting movements that add richness and believability to primary character actions and expressions.
